About Us

Founders:

Marc and Jessica Payte who met while running into one another on multiple hikes, started Pacific Trails Resort in 1999 with the goal of being a premier Northern California glamorous camping destination. The joined forces and created this resort to offer guests a unique lodging experience in private, furnished yurts with ocean views, and provide amenities such as a heated outdoor pool, fine dining, and guided tours.

Our Mission:

To provide a high-quality camping experience and allow as many people as possible to experience the wonders of the Redwoods. The resort aims to combine luxurious comfort with adventure, using their own passion for travel and knowledge of the area. The resort has served over 500,000 customers since it was founded.
