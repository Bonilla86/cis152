Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of trails to explore within Pacific Trails Resort or the adjacent state park, including guided hikes through the redwoods. Got it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayking

Take advantage of the ocean kayaks available for guest use.

Bird Watching

Participate in guided sunrise birdwatching trips offered several times a week. While anytimes is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Tails, we offere guided bird watching trips at sunrise and several times a week.
